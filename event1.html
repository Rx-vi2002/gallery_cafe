Live Music Night at The Gallery Café

Join Us Every Friday for Live Music Performances!

Step into the vibrant atmosphere of The Gallery Café every Friday night and indulge in an evening of live music that will elevate your dining experience. Our Live Music Night is the perfect way to unwind at the end of the week, offering a blend of soulful tunes and delicious cuisine in the heart of Colombo.


Event Details:

Day: Every Friday
Time: 7:00 PM - 12:00 PM
Location: The Gallery Café, Colombo


Experience the magic of live music at The Gallery Café, where talented local artists take the stage to serenade you with a diverse range of musical genres. Whether you’re a fan of jazz, blues, acoustic, or contemporary hits, our live performances cater to all musical tastes. As you enjoy the melodious ambiance, treat your taste buds to our exquisite menu, featuring a selection of delectable dishes and signature cocktails. Our culinary team has curated a menu that perfectly complements the evening’s entertainment, ensuring a memorable dining experience.
