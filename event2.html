Wine Tasting Night at The Gallery Café

Experience a Selection of Fine Wines on Our Tasting Night!

Indulge in an exquisite evening of wine tasting at The Gallery Café, where we bring you the finest selections from around the world. Our Wine Tasting Night is designed for wine enthusiasts and novices alike, offering a unique opportunity to explore a variety of exceptional wines in a sophisticated and relaxed setting.


Event Details:

Day: Monday - Friday
Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Location: The Gallery Café, Colombo


Discover the art of wine tasting at The Gallery Café, where our expert sommeliers guide you through a curated selection of premium wines. Each tasting session is thoughtfully arranged to provide an educational and enjoyable experience, highlighting the distinct flavors, aromas, and histories of each wine. During the evening, you'll have the chance to sample a variety of red, white, and sparkling wines, each paired with gourmet appetizers designed to complement and enhance the tasting experience. Our sommeliers will share insights into the winemaking process, regional characteristics, and tasting techniques, ensuring you leave with a deeper appreciation and understanding of fine wines.

Join us for an unforgettable evening of wine exploration and culinary delight at The Gallery Café. Whether you're a seasoned wine lover or new to the world of wine, our Wine Tasting Night promises an enriching and enjoyable experience.
